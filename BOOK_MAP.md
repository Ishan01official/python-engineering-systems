## My recommended primary book per tier

- **Tier 1 (00–04):** PCC for forward momentum, LP when you need depth.
- **Tier 2 (05–10):** PCC + LP. Glance at EP for "is this idiomatic?"
- **Tier 3 (11–14):** EP becomes the primary book.
- **Tier 4 (15–20):** PfDA is the only book that matters here.
- **Tier 5 (19):** FP, end to end, slowly.
